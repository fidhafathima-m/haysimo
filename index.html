Who We Are

About Haysimo
Water

A celebration of Somali purity, resilience, and the power of nature — bottled for you.

Our Mission

Bringing Nature's
Finest to Your Table

At Haysimo, we believe water is more than a basic necessity — it's a reflection of the land it comes from. Our mission is to harness the incredible purity of Somalia's deep underground water reserves and bring it to homes and businesses across the region and beyond.

Every bottle of Haysimo is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and Somali pride. We source our water responsibly, bottle it with care, and deliver it with the promise that every sip is as nature intended.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01
Purity First
We never compromise on the natural purity of our water. From source to bottle, every step maintains the integrity of what nature provides.
02
Somali Pride
Every bottle celebrates our homeland. We are proud to be a Somali brand, built by Somalis, for Somalis and the world.
03
Sustainability
We draw from nature responsibly, ensuring our water sources remain healthy and abundant for generations to come.
04
Community
We invest in local communities, creating jobs, building infrastructure, and uplifting Puntland's economy through our operations.
05
Transparency
We believe in honest business. Every label tells the truth. Our quality standards are rigorous and open to scrutiny.
06
Excellence
We set the standard for premium water in East Africa. Excellence isn't a goal — it's our baseline expectation for everything we do.

Our Journey

The Beginning
Founded in Bossaso
Haysimo Water was established in the port city of Bossaso, Puntland — tapping into some of Somalia's richest underground water sources.
Product Launch
First Bottles Hit the Market
Our initial product range — the 500ml and 1.5L bottles — launched across Puntland, quickly gaining recognition for exceptional taste and purity.
Expansion
Broader Product Range
We expanded our lineup to include the 250ml personal bottle and the BTC Combo Pack — bringing Haysimo to every occasion and need.
Today & Beyond
Growing with Somalia
Today, Haysimo is one of Somalia's most trusted water brands, with ambitions to serve all of the Horn of Africa and beyond.
